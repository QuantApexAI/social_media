# X/Twitter Platform Compliance Rules

Last updated: 2026-03-29
Version: 1.0

---

## Section 1: Automation Rules

- **Rate limits**: Stay within 1,500 tweets/month on the free tier and no more than 50 API requests per 15-minute window. Exceeding these limits risks account suspension and API access revocation.
- **No duplicate content across accounts**: Do not post identical or near-identical content across multiple accounts. Cross-posting the same message verbatim is considered platform manipulation.
- **No bulk or aggressive follow/unfollow, like, or retweet activity**: Automated mass following, unfollowing, liking, or retweeting is prohibited. Actions must reflect genuine engagement, not algorithmic inflation.
- **No automated engagement farming**: Tactics such as follow-for-follow schemes, retweet-to-win campaigns run through automation, or any coordinated artificial boost of engagement metrics are not permitted.
- **Disclose AI-assisted content**: Where required by X platform policy, label or otherwise disclose content that was generated or materially assisted by AI. Do not misrepresent AI-generated content as wholly human-authored.
- **Minimum 15-minute spacing between posts**: Automated or scheduled posts must be separated by at least 15 minutes to avoid triggering spam detection and to maintain content quality.

---

## Section 2: Content Policy

- **No platform manipulation or coordinated inauthentic behavior**: Do not operate networks of accounts that act in concert to amplify content, inflate metrics, or create false impressions of popularity or consensus.
- **No misleading content about markets, assets, or performance**: Do not publish statements that misrepresent price movements, trading volumes, asset fundamentals, or past/projected performance in a way that could deceive users.
- **No spam or repetitive low-value content**: Avoid posting content that adds no informational value, repeats the same message, or is designed purely to occupy feed space or game the algorithm.
- **No impersonation**: Do not impersonate individuals, organizations, brands, or official accounts. Parody or satire accounts must be clearly labeled as such.
- **No hashtag or trending manipulation**: Do not artificially inject hashtags into unrelated content, coordinate campaigns to force topics to trend, or abuse trending mechanisms to gain unearned visibility.
- **Credit sources and respect intellectual property**: Attribute quotes, data, charts, and analysis to their original sources. Do not reproduce copyrighted material without permission or appropriate fair-use justification.

---

## Section 3: Financial Promotion Rules

- **No misleading financial claims or guaranteed returns**: Do not imply or state that any investment, trade, or asset will achieve specific gains. All forward-looking statements must be clearly qualified as estimates or projections.
- **No pump-and-dump patterns**: Do not publish content designed to artificially inflate the price or perceived value of an asset in order to benefit from a subsequent sale. This includes coordinated buying signals followed by exit announcements.
- **No undisclosed paid promotions**: Any content that is sponsored, incentivized, or produced in exchange for compensation must be clearly disclosed in accordance with applicable advertising standards and X platform policy.
- **No misleading $TICKER cashtag inflation**: Do not use cashtags to associate content with assets in a misleading way, inflate search visibility for a token, or manufacture the appearance of community interest around a symbol.
- **Howey test awareness — avoid framing tokens as securities**: Do not describe or promote tokens in ways that suggest buyers are investing in a common enterprise with an expectation of profit derived from the efforts of others. Consult legal counsel before promoting token offerings.
- **All analysis framed as opinion, not recommendation**: Every piece of market commentary, price analysis, or asset discussion must be presented as the author's opinion only and must not constitute financial advice or a solicitation to buy or sell any asset.
