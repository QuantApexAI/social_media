# Multi-Jurisdiction Financial Disclaimer Rules

Last updated: 2026-03-29
Version: 1.0

## Section 1: US (SEC/FINRA) Guidelines

### What Constitutes Investment Advice

Under US law, investment advice typically requires three elements: a personalized recommendation, compensation, and consideration of the individual's circumstances. QuantApexAI content must avoid all three.

### Rules to Avoid Crossing the Line

- Never use personalized directive language ("you should buy X")
- Never claim to know what will happen ("BTC will hit $100K")
- Never imply guaranteed returns or risk-free opportunities
- Always frame as analysis of publicly available data
- Use conditional language: "if", "watching for", "should X hold Y level"
- Include invalidation conditions in every technical analysis post

### Howey Test Awareness for Crypto

The Howey test determines whether a token qualifies as a security (investment contract). To avoid implying securities status:

- Avoid implying tokens are investment contracts
- Don't suggest profits from efforts of a specific team
- Don't frame token purchases as "investing in" a project's future

## Section 2: UK/EU (FCA/MiFID II) Guidelines

### Financial Promotion Rules

All communications must be fair, clear, and not misleading. Prominent risk warnings are required wherever there is potential for financial loss.

### FCA Crypto Marketing Requirements

- Use required risk warning language for crypto assets
- Make no suitability claims (do not suggest content is appropriate for any individual)
- Avoid urgency or FOMO (fear of missing out) language

### MiFID II Obligations

- Distinguish marketing content from research clearly
- Label all content as opinion, not fact
- Do not target vulnerable audiences

## Classification Note

> QuantApexAI posts are classified as analysis/opinion, not financial promotions under FCA definitions. Posts analyze publicly available data, make no personalized recommendations, receive no issuer compensation, and are framed as analytical observations. When a post could be construed as promotional for a specific asset, a risk awareness note should be considered.
