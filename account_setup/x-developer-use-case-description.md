## Full Version

QuantApexAI is a single-account content publishing tool for the @QuantApexAI X account. It posts original market analysis content (cryptocurrency, stock indices, and forex) using the X API v2.

**What the app does:**
- Posts original tweets and threads containing market recaps, technical analysis, and commentary on notable market movements or AI industry developments
- Uploads chart images alongside analysis posts
- All content is composed with AI assistance, reviewed and approved by a human operator before publishing

**API endpoints used:**
- POST tweets (v2) — to publish approved content
- POST media upload (v1.1) — to attach chart images to tweets
- No read/search endpoints are used for data collection or scraping

**Posting frequency:**
- 2–5 tweets per day, depending on market conditions
- Minimum 15-minute spacing between posts
- Well within the Free tier limit of 1,500 tweets/month

**Content safeguards:**
- All content is framed as analysis and opinion, never as financial advice or personalized recommendations
- A human reviews and approves every post before publishing
- The account bio discloses that content is AI-assisted
- No engagement automation (no automated likes, retweets, follows, or replies)
- No duplicate content across accounts — this is a single-account tool
- No paid promotions or sponsored content

**What we do NOT do:**
- No reading, storing, or redistributing other users' tweet data
- No automated replies, mentions, or interactions with other accounts
- No bulk actions of any kind
- No financial advice, guaranteed return claims, or pump-and-dump patterns

## Short Version (if character-limited)
